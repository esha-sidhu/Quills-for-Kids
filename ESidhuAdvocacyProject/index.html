Support spreading joy to hospital patients while supporting a small business!

About Quills for Kids

Quills for Kids is a movement by Calligraphy By Esha that aims to spread joy to hospital patients by gifting them cards of support and encouragement.

For every card that is bought through the Calligraphy By Esha shop, one card is sent to hospital patients in the community. If you would rather donate without purchasing from the shop, there is an option to donate directly to our cause, and the money will be used to purchase supplies for the cards, without first needing to purchase a card from the shop.

This movement is aimed to spread joy to what can otherwise be a scary, nerve-wracking, and/or painful experience. The core value of Calligraphy By Esha is to spread joy to the community, so this movement is a natural next step.

Impact and Engagement

Meet Sara L

She says that as a cancer patient, things were very rough for her initially in her stay. The cards donated by Quills for Kids brightened her stay.

Meet Alex K

Alex had an accident and had to stay for a prolonged time in the hospital due to complications. They say that the positive messages from the cards from Quills for Kids made them stay more positive.

Meet Clara M

Clara had a rough time with chemotherapy, and she was having a rough time during her stay. Receiving these cards from Quills for Kids brightened her stay and gave her a more positive outlook.

Sign Our Petition

Sign this petition to increase funds for hospitals to increase funding for activities to engage the kids during their stay. There are some grants dedicated to this, and some non-profit organizations who also are trying to collect funds to donate and create grants to fund activities for kids while staying in the hospital.

This is a topic important to Quills for Kids, so we decided to start a petition to get more funding towards fun and stress-relieving activities for kids during their hospital stay, since many activities are not engaging to kids, and the ones that are come from volunteers.
